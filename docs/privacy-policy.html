1. About the App

AI ATS Resume Builder is an ATS resume builder app that helps users create resumes, improve resume content, match resumes with job descriptions, generate resume suggestions, create PDF resumes, and prepare job applications.

The app may include features such as resume creation, AI resume improvement, job description matching, template selection, PDF export, Google login, Supabase account sync, and advertisements through Google AdMob.

The app may use AI features to improve resumes, analyze job descriptions, suggest skills, rewrite bullet points, or generate summaries.

We do not recommend entering sensitive personal information that is not required for your resume.

AI-generated content may not always be perfect. You should review and edit all AI suggestions before using them in a real job application.
